Data Cleaning - Data from DOSM

Cleaning a table that was made for human-readability and transform it to a format that can be used for data analysis.

So for this project, I downloaded this data table from the Department of Statistics Malaysia and found out that they made this table in excel but was meant to be put in a pdf file, hence it was made with a lot of merges and spaces littered everywhere all over the table. Meaning it was not usable for data exploration later.
Yeah, a table with just this much data could be cleaned much faster on Excel but imagine if there are hundreds of thousands of rows.. It would take forever. At least with SQL, we could copy paste the coding over and over again. Heheh
