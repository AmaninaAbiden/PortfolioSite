Is the advertising of e-commerce industry effective in 2019?

An analysis on the performance of e-commerce industry based on their digital advertising activities.

So for this project, I wanted to see what are the efforts that the e-commerce industry has done to digitally promote and create awareness of their products and what are the results (in other word, income) after their marketing efforts, so I consolidated data from the Department of Statistics Malaysia and DATAREPORTAL to do this analysis.
